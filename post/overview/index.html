Overview of the project

Mar 23, 2016  

The DeDiS-lab at EPFL started in August 2015 with the arrival of Bryan Ford at EPFL. With a team of software-engineers, PhD-students and postdocs we’ve been working at different projects, like

  • Cothority - getting signed signatures from 1000s of signers
  • PriFi - Privacy enhanced WiFi using MixNets
